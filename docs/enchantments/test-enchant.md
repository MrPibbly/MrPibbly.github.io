## ⚔️ Razor’s Edge

### 🧾 Overview
**Razor’s Edge** gives a chance to deal bonus damage on hit.  
As the level increases, both the activation chance and damage scale upward.

---

### 📊 Enchantment Stats

| Level | Chance | Damage |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 1     |  10%   |   2    |
| 2     |  20%   |   4    |
| 3     |  30%   |   6    |
| 4     |  40%   |   8    |
| 5     |  50%   |   10   |

---

### 🧠 Scaling Summary

- 📈 **Linear scaling per level**
- 🎯 +10% proc chance per level
- ⚔️ +2 bonus damage per level
- 🧪 Max level: **5**

## Razors Edge

### Description
Gives a **Chance** to deal **Damage** to a player.


| Level | Chance | Damage |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 1     |  10%   |   2    |
| 2     |  20%   |   4    |
| 3     |  30%   |   6    |
| 4     |  40%   |   8    |
| 5     |  50%   |   10   |

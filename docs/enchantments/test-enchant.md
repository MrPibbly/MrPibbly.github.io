## Razors Edge

### Description
Gives a **Chance** to deal **Damage** to a player.


| Level | Chance | Damage |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 1     |  10%   |   2    |
| 2     |  20%   |   4    |
| 3     |  30%   |   6    |
| 4     |  40%   |   8    |
| 5     |  50%   |   10   |


## ⚔️ Razor’s Edge

### 🧾 Overview
**Razor’s Edge** gives a chance to deal bonus damage on hit.  
As the level increases, both the activation chance and damage scale upward.

### 🧠 Scaling Summary

- 📈 **Linear scaling per level**
- 🎯 +10% proc chance per level
- ⚔️ +2 bonus damage per level
- 🧪 Max level: **5**

---

### 🟡 Balance Notes
<abbr title="Designed to remain strong but not overpower PvP combat">Razor’s Edge</abbr> is intended to reward progression without becoming guaranteed burst damage.

- Early levels feel **light but useful**
- Mid levels become **reliably impactful**
- Max level is **strong but not constant**
